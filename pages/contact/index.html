Why form first?

A written start reduces back-and-forth, helps avoid missed details, and gives you a simple way to share what is going on before anyone talks through options with you.

Helpful details to have ready

  • Your attorney's name and firm, if you have it
  • The type of case you are calling about
  • The main question you want answered first
  • The best phone number or email for follow-up

If you prefer to call

Phone support is still available from the header and footer, especially if something is unclear. The form is still the preferred starting point because it gives the team the details they need for follow-up.
